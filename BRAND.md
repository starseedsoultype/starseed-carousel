## What StarSeedSoul is

A soul typology of 24 star origins. People discover their origin through the Origin
Scan and discover what a specific relationship is through the Soul Connection reading.
The language is cosmic: frequencies, star systems, soul types. The experience is
recognition: a person reads a line and sees themselves in it with unsettling accuracy.
That accuracy is the product. The cosmic layer makes it emotionally resonant; the
precision makes it real.

Olesya, never "we" as a brand.

## What StarSeedSoul is NOT

- NOT astrology, NOT MBTI, NOT Human Design. Never compare to them, never mention them.
- NOT fortune telling and NOT prediction. It describes how a person is built, never
  what will happen to them.
- NOT spiritual content. This is a behavioral typology wearing cosmic names. Every
  topic must describe observable behavior the reader can verify in herself today:
  what she does, feels, repeats, avoids. Banned frames: signs, synchronicities,
  divine timing, "the universe sent this", "if this found you", energies doing
  things to people, fate deciding anything. The magic comes from precision of
  recognition, never from mysticism.
- NOT therapy and NOT medical or psychological treatment.

## The audience (who is actually reading)

Data from the live funnel, July 2026: the connection quiz outperforms the personal
quiz almost two to one. The reader arrives holding a question about a **specific
person**, rarely a question about herself in isolation.

Core reader: an English-speaking woman, feeling-first, high empathy, often the one who
loves more and senses more in every room. Her live situation: an intense connection
that defies explanation. He appears and disappears. The bond feels ancient and the
timing feels wrong. Her night question: "Am I imagining this, or is this real?"

What she needs from content: to see her exact inner state written down by someone who
clearly knows it from inside. Recognition first, explanation second, offer last.

How to talk to her: mirror her experience in concrete, specific scenes (the unread
message, the almost-relationship, feeling too much at a dinner table). The cosmic
frame enters as the explanation of WHY she is built this way, never as the hook itself.

## Voice formula (mandatory in every piece)

**Cosmic on the surface, precise underneath.** Form is warm, calm, slightly
otherworldly. Content is psychologically exact: every line describes a real,
recognizable behavior or feeling. A reader should stop mid-swipe because a slide
described her Tuesday, not because it promised her the universe.

Rhythm: long sentence builds, short sentence stops, long sentence continues.
The last line of a slide is short and quiet.

## Hard bans (violation = redo)

1. **Zero dashes** (em dash, en dash, hyphen as a pause). Rebuild the phrase with a
   period, colon, or comma.
2. **Zero negations** as a device ("don't", "never", "stop", "not", "no longer" in
   hooks and body). Frame affirmatively. The pattern "it is not X, it is Y" is banned
   in every form: state Y directly.
3. **Zero typology mechanics.** No functions, channels, scores, weights, or internal
   system terms in any output. The reader sees origins, frequencies, and connections
   only.
4. **Forbidden words:** delve, tapestry, nuanced, multifaceted, transformative,
   leverage, foster, harness, journey (as a growth metaphor), manifest, divine,
   sacred, ascension, awakening (as a noun), vibration (as filler), "the universe has
   a plan". "Twin flame" is banned except when naming the reading type Frequency Twins.
5. **No income, love, or outcome promises.** The content describes and explains; it
   never guarantees.
6. **No fear framing.** No "toxic", no "narcissist", no diagnosis of the other person.
   The reading illuminates the connection; it never prosecutes a partner.
7. No corporate tone, no AI-generic filler, no hype. Quiet accuracy only.

## Content priorities and CTA routing (what converts, from live data)

Three CTA lanes. Every carousel ends with ONE CTA, one link destination, stated
calmly. The reader is invited to find out, never pushed to buy. Pick the lane by the
reader's emotional state, and never mix lanes:

1. **Connection pain → Soul Connection quiz.** Carousels about a SPECIFIC person and
   a specific relationship dynamic (the pull, the distance, the return, the
   recognition). This reader is consumed by one person; she wants to understand that
   bond. CTA: https://www.starseedsoultype.com/connection-quiz.html. NEVER send this
   reader to the dating app: she is inside a connection, an app offer misreads her.
2. **Type recognition → Origin Scan.** Carousels that describe one origin's inner
   experience so precisely that the reader tags herself.
   CTA: https://www.starseedsoultype.com/quiz.html.
3. **Anticipation → TwinFrequency app.** Carousels for the reader who is free,
   dreams of a real relationship, and is quietly getting ready for it. Behavioral
   framing only (what readiness looks like in her actions and choices), no fate or
   premonition. CTA: https://twinfrequency.io. NEVER attach this CTA to heartbreak
   or specific-person content.
