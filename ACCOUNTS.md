# Instagram Accounts — profiles for the carousel generator

The incoming run text starts with `Account: <tag>`. Look the tag up here. The account
profile decides the content lane, the CTA link, the language, and the tone nuance of
the whole carousel and caption. If the tag is missing or unknown, use `main`.

BRAND.md rules apply to every account without exception (behavioral topics only,
zero dashes, zero negations, no typology mechanics, no fate/synchronicity frames).

- **CTA:** the Origin Scan, 7 questions. Link: https://www.starseedsoultype.com/quiz.html

  the return, rereading messages, rehearsing conversations. Highest emotional

- **Reader state:** consumed by one person, wants to understand that bond. NEVER
  offer her the dating app.
- **CTA:** the free TF guide. Link in bio (https://starseedsoul.gumroad.com/l/FreeTFGuide).

## tfapp — @twinf.app (the dating app)

- **CTA:** meet the one who matches your frequency. Link: https://twinfrequency.io
